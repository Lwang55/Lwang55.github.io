What is a CLiCKBOT

A CLiCKBOT is a small robotic tool used to educate children on beginner code. Featuring a silicon outer shell and robust internal casing.

a toy like a hexbug with one wheel, featuring antenna, should look like a toy. AI used: Deepai

Using any paper and a black marker kids are able to draw out tracks and watch the CLiCKBOT solve puzzles and avoid obstacles.

About Us

A guy named Walter Jones. AI- Deepai

CLiCKBOT was founded in 2007 by Walter Jones (seen above) who decided that there needed to be a more portable and convinient way for children to get into technology beyond novelty toys. He sought out to create something which could be easily carried in the pocket and still provide value. And so the click bot was born.

Following his initial prototype. Clickbot has grown to become a staple of computer science programs around the world. Continuing to offer the best means of introducing the wonders of computer programming to children.

How Does It Work?

Through a proprietary internal technology, the Clickbot is able to house several internal sensors as well as 4 smalls motores, enabling it to easily complete its many tasks and offer a variety of subjects to experiment with. The magnetic connection system means that it is also dust and water resistant.

Our Mission

Our mission is to improve the way in which we think about computer science education, and bring tech gadgets from toys discarded after a few days to tools that could serve children throughout their journey. We hope that the growth of Clickbot will mark the beginnings of a new wave of nondisposable and practical toys. built to grow as they grow.

With the rising costs of technology we hope to keep the Clickbot accessible for all in order to ensure a fair and equitable future.
